Who Am I ?

About Me

I am a dedicated Site Reliability Engineer with a strong focus on ensuring the reliability, scalability, and performance of cloud-based systems. My expertise lies in building and maintaining highly available infrastructure, implementing monitoring and observability solutions, and optimizing system performance across cloud platforms.

With hands-on experience in cloud platforms including AWS, Azure, and GCP, I specialize in infrastructure automation, incident response, capacity planning, and implementing SRE best practices. I am passionate about creating resilient systems, reducing toil through automation, and ensuring optimal service reliability and performance in cloud environments.
